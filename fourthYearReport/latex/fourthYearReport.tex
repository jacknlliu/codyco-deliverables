\subsection{Project objectives for the period}

\subsubsection{Overview}

The specificity of CoDyCo relies on the fact that the progress beyond the state of the art is guided by the yearly implementation on the iCub humanoid. Within this context, iCub is a peculiar platform being the only humanoid integrating whole-body distributed force and tactile sensors. In this sense CoDyCo fourth year specific objectives were to design and implement the control of whole-body posture during physical human robot interaction. Other long term objectives involve setting up the necessary infrastructure (human experimental protocols, software infrastructure, learning and control specifications) for leveraging the activities in previous years.
