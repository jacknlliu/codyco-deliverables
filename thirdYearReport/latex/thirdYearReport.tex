\subsection{Project objectives for the period}

\subsubsection{Overview}

The specificity of CoDyCo relies on the fact that the progress beyond the state of the art is guided by the yearly
implementation on the iCub humanoid. Within this context, CoDyCo third year specific objectives were to design and implement
the control of whole-body posture on compliant (non-rigid) contacts. Beyond the activities to achieve this result,
other long term activities have been conducted in preparation for the following years objectives. These activities involve
human experiments, software infrastructure maintenance and the development of learning/control algorithms.
